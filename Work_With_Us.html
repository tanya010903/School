Why Work With Us?

At Government Senior Secondary School Parwanoo, we are committed to providing a supportive and inclusive environment for both our students and staff. We believe in fostering a culture of continuous learning and professional development.

Current Openings

We are always looking for passionate and dedicated individuals to join our team. Check out our current job openings below:

  • Mathematics Teacher
  • Science Teacher
  • English Teacher
  • Physical Education Instructor
  • Administrative Staff

How to Apply

If you are interested in any of the positions listed above, please send your resume and a cover letter to careers@gsssparwanoo.edu. We look forward to hearing from you!

Employee Benefits

We offer a comprehensive benefits package that includes:

  • Professional development opportunities
  • Paid time off
  • Retirement plans
