Admission Process

Eligibility Criteria

Students seeking admission must meet the following criteria:

  • Pre-Primary & Primary: Age 3-10 years
  • Middle School: Completion of previous grade with passing marks
  • Senior Secondary: Minimum 60% in Class 10 (for Science stream), 50% (for Commerce/Arts)
  • Transfer cases: Must provide previous school records

Application Procedure

The application process includes the following steps:

  1. Fill the online admission form
  2. Submit required documents (Birth certificate, Previous academic records, ID proof, Address proof)
  3. Appear for an entrance assessment (if applicable)
  4. Final selection based on merit and seat availability
