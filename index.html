Welcome to Our School

About Us

Government Senior Secondary School Parwanoo is dedicated to providing quality education and fostering a love for learning. Our experienced staff and state-of-the-art facilities create an environment where students can thrive.

Our Programs

We offer a variety of academic programs including Science, Commerce, and Arts streams. Our curriculum is designed to prepare students for higher education and successful careers.

Extracurricular Activities

Our school provides numerous extracurricular activities such as sports, music, dance, and drama. These activities help in the holistic development of our students.

Community Engagement

We believe in giving back to the community. Our students participate in various community service projects, helping them develop a sense of responsibility and empathy.
